About the Game

"Name of the Game" is an immersive first-person horror experience that plunges players into a terrifying world filled with danger, mystery, and intrigue. Set in an abandoned environment, you must navigate through dark, eerie locations while avoiding deadly creatures and uncovering the secrets of a mad scientist's twisted experiments. The game challenges players with various puzzles, terrifying encounters, and an atmosphere of dread. The story unfolds as you search for another human being, and every step you take brings you closer to the horrifying truth about the scientist and his experiments.

Story

You wake up in an eerie, abandoned world, with no recollection of how you arrived. The only trace of life is the voice of a scientist calling out to you through distorted radio transmissions. You must traverse through dark forests, decaying buildings, and haunted ruins in search of another human, all while being hunted by terrifying creatures created by the mad scientist. As you explore, you uncover fragments of the scientist’s twisted research and horrific experiments, leading you to believe that there may be more to the world’s abandonment than you originally thought. Will you survive long enough to uncover the full story?
